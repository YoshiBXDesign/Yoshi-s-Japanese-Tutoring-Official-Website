Privacy Policy

Purpose of This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect information in the course of providing Japanese tutoring services. It is written to be clear and direct, so that you can understand what happens to your data without needing legal training.

Data Controller

For the purposes of data protection, the data controller is: Yoshi’s Japanese Tutoring, Houston, Texas, United States. All references to “I”, “me”, or “my” refer to Yoshi’s Japanese Tutoring as an independently operated tutoring service.

Information I Collect

I collect and process only the information that is necessary to deliver the service with quality and care, including:

Identification details you choose to share, such as your name and contact information.

Information about your goals, current level, and learning history.

Communication records, such as emails and consultation notes.

Limited technical information related to website usage, such as access time and basic analytics, where enabled. I do not request or store payment card details directly on this site; if third‑party payment tools are used, they operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used for specific, legitimate purposes:

To understand your objectives and determine whether we are a good fit.

To design and adjust a personalized learning plan.

To communicate with you about scheduling, expectations, and ongoing progress.

To maintain internal records necessary for responsible business operations and compliance. I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Legal Basis for Processing

Depending on your location, personal data may be processed under different legal bases, including: Your consent, the performance of a contract, and my legitimate interest in running a stable, secure, and effective tutoring service. Where consent is the basis, you may withdraw it at any time.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Your personal information is shared only where necessary and appropriate, for example: Email and calendar providers, Video conferencing tools, and Payment platforms. I do not authorize them to use your information for their own marketing.

Data Retention

I keep your information only for clearly defined periods:

Learning and communication records are retained for two years after our last interaction.

Financial and invoicing data are retained for six years, in line with accounting obligations. When these periods expire, information is deleted or securely anonymized.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights over your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your data. Contact me using the details on the Contact page to exercise these rights.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect your information. I treat the protection of your data as an essential part of running a professional tutoring practice.

International Considerations

The service is operated from the United States, and data may be processed or stored there. By communicating with me, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred across borders.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions about this Policy, contact me using the details on the Contact page.
