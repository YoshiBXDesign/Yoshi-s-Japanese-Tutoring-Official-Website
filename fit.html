Who It’s For

Designed For Certain Learners

• People preparing for a real chapter in Japan: a move, a role, a business relationship that deserves to go well.

• Learners who see Japanese as an investment in their work, identity, and future—not as a casual hobby.

• Those who value thoughtful guidance, clear expectations, and a tutor who treats their time seriously.

When Another Option Is Better

• If your plans are still undefined and you are simply “curious” about Japanese, lighter options will serve you better.

• If you are looking for something easy, inexpensive, or instantly gratifying, this program will feel misaligned.

• If you prefer to be carried rather than to participate actively, a self‑paced app or group class may be preferable.
